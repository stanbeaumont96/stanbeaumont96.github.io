Welcome to Learn Russian with Stan!

Всем Привет!
Learn Russian with Stan is a language learning website aimed at aiding people studying Russian, who have at least some experience in reading Russian and who understand the basic concepts of the language, to understand some seemingly complex areas of Russian grammar. Each topic covered throughout this website will include text informing the learner about the particular aspect of grammar and will be followed by exercises which reinforce the knowledge aquired through reading the lesson.

The topics covered on this website include:

  • Russian case endings
  • Verbal aspect
  • Verbs of motion

    What makes Learn Russian with Stan different from other language learning sites?

    Throughout my years of studying Russian, it is not only my language skills that have advanced, but also my understanding of how to learn a language. Experience has shown that knowledge is assimilated more proficiently if it is presented in a fun, interactive form. Using interactive exercises based on real Russian media; such as songs and film clips,this site aims to entertain the learner as well as to inform them.
